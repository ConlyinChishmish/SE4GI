BinEco, be clean!


BinEco is a web app that will help you in managing the distribution and organization of the bins in your city.

You will be able to:

  • Visualize the distribution of the litter
  • Visualize the distribution of the bins classified as critical or non critical
  • Show the statistical analisys and update the bin by clicking on it in the map
  • Add a new bin
