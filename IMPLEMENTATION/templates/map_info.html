How to use the interactive map

In the page just opened, you can see two maps:

  • on the left the map of litter points retreived from Epicollect5 project
  • on the right the map of the bins colored in red if they are critical and in green if they are not

Passing on the points some useful information about the data are shown

By clicking on the bin's point you can access the analysis page by inserting the ID of the chosen bin
